Game Concepts

Game Ideas From My Imagination

I may be in Class 10, but my mind is already full of game concepts. These are idea-level "projects" — the games I'd love to build one day as I keep learning coding and design.

01
Runner / Cyberpunk

Neon Runner City

Endless runner with story-based missions.

You play as a courier in a neon futuristic city, dodging traffic, jumping rooftops, and unlocking secret routes. Speed meets storytelling.

  • Core: Smooth parkour + branching missions
  • Mechanics: Slide, wall-run, slow-motion jumps
  • Inspiration: Mirror's Edge + Cyberpunk
💡 Concept Runner / Action
02
Strategy / MOBA

Sky Chess Arena

Strategy game mixing chess and arena combat.

Classic chess pieces come to life in a floating arena. Each piece has special abilities and cooldowns. Think like a chess master, fight like an arena champion.

  • Core: Strategy + real-time action
  • Mechanics: Ability combos & special moves
  • Inspiration: Chess, MOBAs, Hero Shooters
💡 Concept Strategy / Action
03
Puzzle / Time

Puzzle of Time

Story-driven puzzle game about time loops.

You wake up in the same day again and again. Each loop lets you keep knowledge but not items. Use clues from previous loops to solve deeper puzzles.

  • Core: Learning-based progression
  • Mechanics: Timelines, clues, hidden routes
  • Inspiration: Outer Wilds + 12 Minutes
💡 Concept Puzzle / Narrative
04
RPG / Open World

Shadow Realm Chronicles

Dark fantasy RPG with moral decisions.

An open-world RPG where every major choice creates ripple effects across the world. Join the light, embrace the shadow, or forge your own path between both.

  • Core: Branching narrative + open exploration
  • Mechanics: Morality meter, faction alliances
  • Inspiration: The Witcher + Dark Souls
🖊️ Designing RPG / Fantasy
05
Survival / Sci-Fi

Last Signal

Solo survival on an alien crash site.

You crash-land on an alien planet with one broken radio. Explore, scavenge, craft, and build a signal tower — all while fending off evolving alien threats that adapt to your tactics.

  • Core: Adaptive AI + resource management
  • Mechanics: Crafting, base building, stealth
  • Inspiration: Subnautica + Alien Isolation
🖊️ Designing Survival / Sci-Fi
