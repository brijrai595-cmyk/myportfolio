Game Concepts

Game Ideas From My Imagination

Inspired by anime, Pokémon, and classic games — these are my original game concepts that I'd love to build.

01
Runner / Cyberpunk

Neon Runner City

Endless runner with story-based missions.

Play as a courier in a neon futuristic city, dodging traffic, jumping rooftops — inspired by cyberpunk anime aesthetics.

  • Core: Parkour + branching missions
  • Mechanics: Slide, wall-run, slow-motion
  • Vibe: Ghost in the Shell meets Mirror's Edge
💡 Concept Runner / Action
02
Strategy / MOBA

Sky Chess Arena

Strategy meets arena combat.

Chess pieces come alive with special abilities. Think Pokémon battles meets chess — every piece has a unique move set.

  • Core: Strategy + real-time action
  • Mechanics: Ability combos & cooldowns
  • Vibe: Chess + MOBAs + Pokémon
💡 Concept Strategy / Action
03
Puzzle / Time

Puzzle of Time

Time-loop puzzle adventure.

Wake up in the same day again and again. Use knowledge from previous loops to solve deeper puzzles — anime-style mystery narrative.

  • Core: Learning-based progression
  • Mechanics: Timelines, clues, hidden routes
  • Vibe: Steins;Gate meets Outer Wilds
💡 Concept Puzzle / Narrative
04
RPG / Anime-Inspired

Shadow Realm Chronicles

Dark fantasy RPG — anime arcs with player choices.

An open-world RPG where story arcs unfold like anime seasons. Every choice reshapes alliances. Inspired by SAO, DanMachi, and Re:Zero.

  • Core: Branching narrative + anime arcs
  • Mechanics: Morality system, faction fights
  • Vibe: SAO + Elden Ring + Demon Slayer
🖊️ Designing RPG / Fantasy
05
Monster Capture / RPG

Spirit Catcher

Original monster-capture RPG inspired by Pokémon.

A creature-collection RPG set in an ancient spirit world. Capture elemental spirits, train them, and battle other spirit tamers across a huge open map.

  • Core: Capture, evolve, battle spirits
  • Mechanics: Elemental weaknesses & synergies
  • Vibe: Pokémon + Naruto's Summoning Jutsu
🖊️ Designing Monster RPG
